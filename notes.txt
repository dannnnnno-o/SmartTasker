|| GradeX ||

GradeX is an Automatic Grading System that allows students to manage their schoolworks efficiently by providing them a space where they can manage subjects and specific tasks.

Features:
    Workspace
    Sortable To-do List 
    Numerical Grade to 11-point system conversion
    Automatic Grading System (AGS)

Limitations: 
    Manual input for subjects, tasks, and rubrics
    No online support
